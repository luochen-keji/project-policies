1. Minimal Collection Principle: We only collect information necessary to provide software functions, refrain from collecting irrelevant personal information excessively, and will not obtain your consent through misleading, fraudulent or coercive means.<br>
2. Local-First Principle: All user data of the current version is stored locally on your device, and will not be actively uploaded to any server. We cannot access or obtain your local data.<br>
3. Encryption Protection Principle: Local data is stored with industry-standard encryption algorithms to protect the security of your documents and prevent tampering and leakage.<br>
4. Transparent Iteration Principle: When new functions such as login and cloud synchronization are launched in the future, We will clearly inform you of the scope, purpose and storage method of information collection. Relevant services will only be provided after obtaining your explicit consent, and new functions will not be mandatory.<br>
5. User Autonomy & Control Principle: You have full control over your document data. You may view, edit and delete content at any time, and all data storage and deletion operations are controlled independently by You.
## 2. Information Processing Rules for Current Version
(No login, no cloud synchronization, fully free)
### 2.1 Information Collection
The current version is completely free, with no registration or login required.We do not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, ID number, email, unique device identifier, geographic location and other sensitive data.No third-party statistics or tracking SDKs are integrated. We will not request any sensitive device permissions such as contacts, albums, microphones or cameras.<br>
1. Only the following local data will be generated and stored on your device during use (stored locally only, beyond our access or control):
User-created content including manuscripts, notes, templates and formatting settings (collectively referred to as "User Documents");

3. Application operation logs (only attached when you actively submit feedback). Logs only record software operating status without personal information or document content, and are solely used for troubleshooting and functional optimization.
### 2.2 Information Usage
Local data collected will only be used for the following purposes, without unauthorized analysis, sharing or commercial utilization:

2. To record your usage preferences such as fonts and themes, improving operating experience and response efficiency;
3. To fix bugs and optimize functions when you voluntarily submit feedback with operation logs.
### 2.3 Data Storage & Protection
1. Storage Method: All user data is encrypted and stored locally on your computer or mobile device. No data will be uploaded to the cloud or our servers, and all data is fully controlled by You.
2. Storage Period: The data retention period is determined by You. You may delete single documents or clear all local data at any time. Deleted data can only be restored through your local device backup (if enabled), and We cannot recover or access deleted content.
3. Security Protection: We adopt industry-standard encryption technologies (such as AES) to protect local data from tampering and leakage. No system or device is absolutely secure. 4. Data Destruction: All local user data will be deleted upon software uninstallation if not backed up in advance. We will not retain any residual user data.
### 2.4 Information Sharing & Disclosure
For the current version, We do not collect or store any of your personal information or user documents. Accordingly, no information sharing, transfer or public disclosure will occur.
We will only cooperate and provide relevant information when required by laws, regulations or legitimate official government requests. Given that no user data is collected in the current version, no content can be provided. We will fully protect your privacy within the scope permitted by law.
